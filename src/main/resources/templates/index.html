GMK BLUE MOUNTAIN

Blue Mountains is a mountainous region and mountain range in New South Wales, Australia. Blue Mountains is designated as Blue Mountains National Park along with the surrounding area. In addition, this national park and the surrounding national parks are collectively called the Greater Blue Mountains Area and are registered in the UNESCO World Heritage List. The cities near Blue Mountains include the City of Blue Mountains, City of Hawkesbury, City of Lisse and Oberon Shire.
